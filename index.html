Build Your Own Furniture

Introduction

Affordable & Attractive
Home Furniture

If you don't consider yourself "handy" or you have never used a screwdriver, drilled screw holes, or measured before—you can build inexpensive basic pieces of furniture for your needs.

All the furniture is designed for the most economical budget. Crafting your own furniture is easier than you think. Most of the projects are assembled and finished on a single weekend.

A workspace can be sawhorses with a two-by-four foot plank over them or an old table. The garage, backyard, basement, or any uncluttered place can be the location. Now you become producer, not only a user—you will gain confidence with each and every new skill you learn.

Minimal Tools

Local Wood

Wood harvested in the USA, available locally in over 5,000 home centers, precut and ready to assemble at great cost savings.

Portable

Disassembles into small portable packages that fit in car trunks for transportation to a new location, then reassembled.

Complete Line

Living room, dining room, bedroom, kitchen, and study—a full coordinated furniture line at minimum affordable cost.

Custom Finish

Select your own stain colors and finishes. Be proud of the design quality and harmonious style you create.

Family Activity

Your spouse and kids can help. Kids enjoy quality time with parents, creating lasting memories together.
